The tuner isn't hearing my guitar

Retune needs microphone access. Check Settings → Retune → Microphone is on, and play close to the phone in a quiet-ish room.

How do I change tuning?

Tap the tuning name at the top (e.g. "Standard ⌄") and pick from the list — Drop D, DADGAD, open tunings, and more.

Can I add my own custom tuning?

Not yet — custom presets are planned for a future update. v1 ships with the built-in tunings.
