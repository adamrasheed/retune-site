Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Retune collects nothing. There is no account, no tracking, and no data leaves your device. Ever.

Microphone

Retune uses your device's microphone to detect the pitch of your strings in real time. Audio is analyzed on your device as it happens and is never recorded, saved, stored, or transmitted anywhere. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

Data we collect

None. Retune does not collect, store, or share any personal information, usage data, or analytics. There are no accounts to create and no sign-in.

Third parties

Retune contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. Your information is never sold or shared because none is collected.

Children

Retune does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
