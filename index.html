What it does
01

Tracks the string you're playing

Play any string and the tuner locks to the nearest target in the preset, then holds it steady while you adjust, with no jumping between notes as you get close.

02

Alternate tunings, already set up

Nine tunings built in: standard, dropped, and open. Switch between them in a tap instead of re-entering notes every time.

03

Runs entirely on device

No account, no network, no tracking. It opens straight to the tuner and works anywhere, including offline.

04

Reads in cents

A cents meter plus a short haptic the moment a string lands in tune, within five cents of the target.
